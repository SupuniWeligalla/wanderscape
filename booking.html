Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my trip?

For popular destinations during peak seasons (December-January, summer months), we recommend booking at least 6-8 months in advance to secure the best accommodations and experiences. For off-season travel, 3-4 months ahead is typically sufficient.

Are flights included in your packages?

Our standard packages do not include international flights, allowing flexibility for travelers from different departure locations. However, we can arrange flights at your request. Domestic flights within multi-destination packages are typically included.

Can you accommodate special dietary requirements?

Yes, we work closely with hotels and restaurants to accommodate dietary restrictions and preferences. Please note your requirements in the "Special Requests" section of the booking form, and we'll ensure they're communicated to all relevant providers.

Do I need a visa for these destinations?

Visa requirements depend on your nationality. UAE (Dubai) offers visa on arrival for many nationalities. Maldives provides a 30-day tourist visa on arrival for all nationalities. Singapore has visa-free arrangements with many countries. We'll provide detailed visa information specific to your nationality after booking.

What's your approach to sustainable tourism?

We're committed to sustainable tourism practices, partnering with eco-conscious accommodations, supporting local communities, and minimizing environmental impact. We offer options for carbon offsetting and can suggest eco-friendly activities and accommodations upon request.
