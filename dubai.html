About Dubai

Dubai is a city of superlatives, home to the world's tallest building, largest mall, and some of the most luxurious hotels on the planet. Located in the United Arab Emirates, Dubai has transformed from a small fishing village to a global metropolis known for its ultramodern architecture, vibrant nightlife, and world-class shopping experiences.

The city offers a unique blend of traditional Arabian culture and futuristic innovation, making it a must-visit destination for travelers seeking both luxury and adventure.

Weather & Best Time to Visit

Dubai has a desert climate with extremely hot summers and mild winters. The best time to visit is between November and April when temperatures are pleasant for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

Winter (Nov-Feb)

Perfect weather with temperatures ranging from 20-25°C (68-77°F). Ideal for outdoor activities and desert safaris.

Spring (Mar-Apr)

Comfortable temperatures between 25-32°C (77-90°F). Great for beach activities and city exploration.

Summer (May-Sep)

Extremely hot with temperatures often exceeding 40°C (104°F). Best for indoor activities like shopping and museum visits.

Autumn (Oct)

Temperatures begin to cool down to 30-35°C (86-95°F). A good time to visit as tourist crowds are smaller.

Top Places to Visit in Dubai

  • Burj Khalifa

    The world's tallest building at 828 meters (2,717 feet), offering breathtaking views from its observation decks. Visit at sunset for a spectacular panorama of the city as it transforms from day to night.

    Don't Miss: The observation decks on the 124th, 125th, and 148th floors.

  • The Dubai Mall

    One of the world's largest shopping malls, featuring over 1,200 retail outlets, an indoor theme park, an ice rink, and the famous Dubai Aquarium and Underwater Zoo.

    Don't Miss: The Dubai Fountain show outside the mall, with performances set to music every 30 minutes in the evenings.

  • Palm Jumeirah

    An artificial archipelago in the shape of a palm tree, home to luxury hotels, upscale apartments, and pristine beaches. The iconic Atlantis, The Palm resort is located at the crown of the palm.

    Don't Miss: A relaxing stay at one of the luxury resorts or a visit to Aquaventure Waterpark at Atlantis.

  • Dubai Desert Safari

    Experience the traditional Bedouin way of life with a desert safari tour. Activities include dune bashing, camel riding, sandboarding, and enjoying a barbecue dinner under the stars with traditional entertainment.

    Don't Miss: The magical sunset over the golden sand dunes and the stargazing opportunities away from the city lights.

  • Dubai Marina

    A vibrant waterfront community with stunning skyscrapers, luxury yachts, and a scenic 7km promenade. Perfect for dining, shopping, and enjoying the vibrant nightlife.

    Don't Miss: A dinner cruise along the marina or a leisurely walk along the Marina Walk at sunset.

Dubai Culinary Experience

Dubai offers a diverse culinary scene with influences from around the world, as well as traditional Emirati cuisine. From street food to Michelin-starred restaurants, there's something for every palate.

Local Dishes to Try

  • Al Machboos: Spiced rice with meat, similar to biryani
  • Shawarma: A popular street food of grilled meat wrapped in flatbread
  • Knafeh: A sweet cheese pastry soaked in sugar syrup
  • Luqaimat: Sweet dumplings drizzled with date syrup
  • Arabic Coffee (Gahwa): Served with dates, a symbol of hospitality

Dining Experiences

  • Luxury Dining: World-class restaurants helmed by celebrity chefs
  • Dhow Cruise: Dinner on a traditional wooden boat
  • Desert Dining: BBQ dinner under the stars in a desert camp
  • Friday Brunch: An institution in Dubai, offering lavish buffets
  • Food Tours: Explore the old town's hidden culinary gems

Dubai Travel Tips

Planning

Book attractions like Burj Khalifa in advance to secure your preferred time slot. Consider a 5-day stay minimum to experience the highlights.

Budget

Dubai can be expensive, but there are options for all budgets. Public transport is affordable, and many attractions offer combo tickets for savings.

Transportation

The Dubai Metro is clean, efficient, and connects major attractions. Taxis are plentiful and relatively affordable for short distances.

Cultural Etiquette

Dress modestly in public places, especially religious sites. Public displays of affection should be limited. Always ask before photographing locals.
