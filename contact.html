Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a trip through WanderScape?

You can book a trip through our website by visiting the "Book Now" page or contacting our team directly for personalized assistance with your travel arrangements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For certain destinations, we also offer payment plans to make your dream vacation more accessible.

Can I modify or cancel my booking?

Yes, most bookings can be modified or canceled according to the specific terms and conditions. We recommend reviewing the cancellation policy for your booking or contacting our customer service team for assistance.

Do you provide travel insurance?

While we don't directly sell travel insurance, we strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance for your trip. We can suggest reputable providers that offer coverage suitable for your destination and activities.

How far in advance should I book my trip?

For popular destinations during peak season, we recommend booking at least 6-8 months in advance. For off-season travel or less busy destinations, 3-4 months ahead is typically sufficient to secure good rates and availability.
